Weapons Overview

Half-Life features a diverse arsenal of 14 distinct weapons, ranging from simple melee tools to powerful alien technology. Gordon Freeman begins the game with only a crowbar and progressively acquires more powerful firearms as he fights through Black Mesa and Xen.

Weapon Categories

Melee
  • Crowbar — The iconic melee weapon, unlimited use
Firearms (Human)
  • Glock 17 — Standard 9mm pistol
  • MP5 — 9mm submachine gun with grenade launcher
  • .357 Magnum — High-powered revolver
  • Shotgun — Lever-action 12-gauge
  • Crossbow — Sniper weapon with laser sight
Explosives
  • RPG — Rocket-propelled grenade launcher
  • Hand Grenade — Fragmentation grenade
  • Satchel Charge — Remote-detonated explosive
  • Trip Mine — Laser-triggered explosive
Alien / Energy Weapons
  • Snark — Living alien projectile
  • Hornet Gun — Fires alien hornets
  • Tau Cannon — Charged particle beam
  • Gluon Gun — Continuous energy beam

Ammunition Types

  • 9mm Rounds — Used by Glock 17 and MP5
  • .357 Rounds — Used by .357 Magnum
  • 12 Gauge Shells — Used by Shotgun
  • Crossbow Bolts — Used by Crossbow
  • Rockets — Used by RPG
  • Energy Cells — Used by Tau Cannon and Gluon Gun
  • Hornets — Used by Hornet Gun (regenerates over time)

Weapon Notes

  • The crowbar is the most recognizable weapon in the series.
  • The Tau Cannon can be charged for more damage.
  • The Gluon Gun drains energy cells rapidly.
  • Snarks attack friend and foe alike.
