Chapter List

Half-Life is divided into 18 chapters (19 in the PlayStation 2 version). Each chapter represents a distinct phase of Gordon Freeman's journey through Black Mesa and Xen.

Complete Chapter Index

  1. Black Mesa Inbound — Gordon arrives at work via the tram system
  2. Anomalous Materials — The resonance cascade
  3. Unforeseen Consequences — The alien invasion begins
  4. Office Complex — Navigating the devastated office areas
  5. We Got Hostiles — The HECU arrives
  6. Blast Pit — The Tentacle and the rocket engine
  7. Chapter 7: Power Up — Restoring power to the transit system
  8. Chapter 8: On a Rail — The railway system through Black Mesa
  9. Chapter 9: Resonance — The surface and the Apache
  10. Chapter 10: Forget About Freeman — The dam and the Ichthyosaur
  11. Chapter 11: Lambda Core — The surface approach to Lambda Complex
  12. Chapter 12: Surface Tension — The final HECU encounters
  13. Chapter 13: Forget About Freeman (repeat) — Continued surface combat
  14. Chapter 14: Lambda Core — The Lambda Complex and teleporter
  15. Chapter 15: Xen — The alien borderworld
  16. Chapter 16: Gonarch's Lair — The headcrab mother
  17. Chapter 17: Interloper — The Vortigaunt factories
  18. Chapter 18: Nihilanth — The final battle

Chapter Notes

  • The first chapter has no enemies — it's purely atmospheric.
  • Chapter 13 shares its name with Chapter 10 (a known bug).
  • The PlayStation 2 version includes a 19th chapter.
  • Chapters are loaded as separate map files (no seamless streaming).
