Overview

Black Mesa Inbound is the introductory chapter of Half-Life. Gordon Freeman rides the tram system into the Black Mesa Research Facility. This chapter has no enemies or combat — it serves as a atmospheric tour of the facility and establishes the setting.

Key Events

  • Gordon rides the tram through the facility's transit system.
  • The player sees various facility areas: hangars, test labs, security checkpoints.
  • The chapter ends as Gordon arrives at the Anomalous Materials drop-off point.

Weapons Found

None — this is a non-combat chapter.

Enemies Encountered

None.

Notes

  • The tram ride is one of the most famous introductions in video game history.
  • Players can look around freely during the tram ride.
  • The G-Man is briefly visible on the platform before Gordon's tram arrives.
  • The chapter is essentially a "on rails" experience.
