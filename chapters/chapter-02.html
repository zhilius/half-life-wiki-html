Overview

Gordon arrives at the Anomalous Materials Lab, dons the H.E.V. Suit, and participates in the experiment that triggers the resonance cascade. This chapter marks the point of no return — the disaster that sets the entire game in motion.

Key Events

  • Gordon is directed to the H.E.V. suit room and suits up.
  • The test chamber: Gordon inserts the Xen crystal into the spectrometer beam.
  • The resonance cascade — dimensional rifts tear open across the facility.
  • Gordon escapes the test chamber and acquires the crowbar and Glock 17.

Weapons Found

  • Crowbar
  • Glock 17

Enemies Encountered

None (the chapter ends as the invasion begins).

Notes

  • The H.E.V. suit room includes a charging station.
  • The G-Man is visible in the test chamber control room.
  • The laboratory equipment is based on real CERN particle accelerator designs.
