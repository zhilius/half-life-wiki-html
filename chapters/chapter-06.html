Overview

Gordon reaches the rocket test facility where a giant Tentacle blocks the path. To proceed, Gordon must activate fuel and coolant pumps, then ignite the rocket engine to incinerate the creature. One of the most memorable set-piece encounters in the game.

Key Events

  • Gordon enters the Blast Pit rocket test chamber.
  • The Tentacle attacks — it senses vibrations.
  • Gordon must crouch-walk to avoid detection.
  • Activate the fuel pump, coolant pump, and ignition sequence.
  • The rocket engine fires, killing the Tentacle.
