Overview

Alien Controllers are floating Xen creatures with powerful psychic abilities. They hover above the ground and fire energy projectiles at the player. They are the most common ranged enemies encountered in Xen and can be dangerous in groups.

Enemy Specifications

Controller Stats
CategoryXen Psychic

Damage10 per energy projectile

First SeenChapter 15: Xen

Combat Strategies

  • Use the Gluon Gun or Tau Cannon for quick kills.
  • Take cover from their energy projectiles.
  • They float — aim slightly above center mass.
  • Kill them before they can group up.
