Overview

The Nihilanth is the final boss of Half-Life. A gigantic, floating psychic entity, the Nihilanth is the ruler of Xen and the mastermind behind the alien invasion of Earth. It is responsible for enslaving the Vortigaunts and directing the Xen forces through the dimensional rifts opened by the resonance cascade.

Behavior

The Nihilanth floats at the center of a large chamber. It attacks with slow-moving energy orbs that require precise timing to avoid. It periodically teleports around the arena and summons Alien Controllers and other minions to assist. Its weak point is the glowing membrane on its chest.

Combat Strategies

  • Target the glowing chest area for maximum damage.
  • Dodge energy orbs — they track but are slow.
  • Use the Gluon Gun and Tau Cannon for sustained damage.
  • Kill summoned minions quickly to avoid being overwhelmed.
  • Collect energy cells from crystals around the arena.

Post-Battle

After the Nihilanth's defeat, the chamber begins to collapse. The G-Man appears, congratulating Gordon and offering him a choice: accept employment with the G-Man's mysterious employers, or face death. This leads to the iconic ending sequence where Gordon is placed into stasis.
