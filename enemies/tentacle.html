Overview

The Tentacle is a massive alien organism encountered in Chapter 6: Blast Pit. It is blind but senses vibrations through the ground. Its massive appendage can kill the player instantly if it strikes. The Tentacle cannot be killed directly — the player must activate a rocket engine to incinerate it.

Behavior

The Tentacle has multiple heads that emerge from a pit. It senses vibrations — the player must move quietly (crouch-walk) to avoid detection. If the Tentacle detects movement, it slams down, killing instantly. The player must navigate the room, activate fuel and coolant pumps, and ignite the rocket engine while avoiding the Tentacle's strikes.

Combat Strategies

  • Crouch-walk to avoid detection.
  • Throw objects to distract the Tentacle.
  • Do not run — vibrations attract it.
  • Complete the rocket sequence to kill it.

Quick Facts
