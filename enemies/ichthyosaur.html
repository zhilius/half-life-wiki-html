Overview

The Ichthyosaur is a large aquatic alien predator found in flooded sections of Black Mesa. It is the only exclusively water-based enemy in the game. Fast and aggressive in water, it can easily kill Gordon if he is caught without oxygen or sufficient firepower.

Behavior

Ichthyosaurs patrol underwater areas. They are extremely fast swimmers and will aggressively pursue the player. They attack with a powerful bite. On land, they flop helplessly and can be easily avoided or killed.

Combat Strategies

  • Stay out of the water when possible.
  • Use the crossbow or RPG for underwater engagement.
  • Surface regularly to replenish H.E.V. oxygen.
  • If beached, an ichthyosaur is vulnerable and slow.

Quick Facts

  • Only fully aquatic enemy in the game.
  • Extremely fast in water.
  • Helpless on land.
  • Can be avoided in most encounters.
