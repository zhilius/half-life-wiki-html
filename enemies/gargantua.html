Overview

The Gargantua is a massive, heavily armored alien behemoth from Xen. It appears twice in the game: on the surface level and in the Lambda Complex. The Gargantua is essentially a mini-boss — it cannot be killed with small arms fire and requires heavy weapons to defeat.

Behavior

Gargantuas stomp heavily toward the player, dealing massive damage on contact. They are immune to most small-arms fire. They can charge at high speed and will pursue the player through multiple rooms. Their armored hide deflects bullets.

Combat Strategies

  • Use RPG rockets to stun and damage the Gargantua.
  • The Tau Cannon (charged) is also effective.
  • Lead the Gargantua into environmental hazards when possible.
  • Never engage in close quarters — maintain distance.
