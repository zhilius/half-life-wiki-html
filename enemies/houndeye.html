Overview

The Houndeye is a three-legged alien creature from Xen. It moves in packs and attacks by emitting a damaging sonic shockwave from its large central eye. Houndeyes are highly social — they bark to communicate and become aggressive when their pack is threatened.

Behavior

Houndeyes patrol in groups. They stand on three legs and hop toward enemies. Their sonic blast is an area-of-effect attack that damages everything in a cone in front of them. When one houndeye is attacked, the entire pack becomes hostile. They are most dangerous in groups where their sonic blasts overlap.

Combat Strategies

  • Pick them off from range before they can group up.
  • The MP5 is effective for medium-range engagements.
  • Dodge sideways to avoid the sonic blast cone.
  • Always check for additional pack members nearby.
