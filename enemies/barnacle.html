Overview

The Barnacle is a stationary alien creature that attaches to ceilings. It hangs a long, sticky tongue that dangles to the floor. When the player or an NPC touches the tongue, it retracts, pulling the victim upward into the Barnacle's mouth to be devoured.

Behavior

Barnacles are stationary. They drop their tongue and wait for prey. The tongue is sensitive to touch — brushing against it triggers the retraction. The Barnacle slowly pulls victims upward, dealing damage over time. If the victim escapes or kills the Barnacle before being fully consumed, they survive.

Combat Strategies

  • Always look up when entering new rooms.
  • Shoot the Barnacle's mouth to kill it instantly.
  • The crowbar can free you if grabbed early.

Quick Facts

  • Attached to ceilings — always look up.
  • Tongue is one-shot kill if fully retracted.
  • Can be used as an improvised elevator.
  • Killed by a single shot to the mouth.
