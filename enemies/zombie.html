Overview

A Zombie is created when a Headcrab successfully attaches to a human victim. The headcrab takes control of the host body, which lurches forward clumsily, attacking with its arms. Zombies are slow but dangerous in close quarters.

Behavior

Zombies shamble toward the player and attack with melee swipes. They often appear in groups and can corner the player in tight spaces. The headcrab on top remains active and will sometimes disengage to attack separately if the zombie body is destroyed.

Combat Strategies

  • Use the shotgun at close range for maximum damage.
  • Keep distance — their melee attacks are powerful.
  • Headshots can kill the headcrab without destroying the body.
  • Listen for their distinctive moans to detect them early.

Quick Facts

  • Created by a headcrab attaching to a human.
  • The headcrab can detach from a destroyed zombie.
  • Slow but hits hard in melee.
  • Often found in dark, confined areas.
