Overview

The Headcrab is the most iconic and frequently encountered alien enemy in Half-Life. These small, spider-like creatures from Xen leap at human faces and attach themselves to victims' heads, transforming them into Zombies. Headcrabs are the first alien enemy encountered after the resonance cascade.

Behavior

Headcrabs scuttle along floors and walls, leaping at their target when within range. A successful headcrab leap onto a human head will create a Zombie. Headcrabs cannot infect Gordon (the H.E.V. suit prevents attachment) but their claw attacks still deal damage.

Combat Strategies

  • Kill headcrabs before they leap — they are vulnerable in mid-air.
  • The crowbar is very effective against headcrabs.
  • Listen for their distinctive chirping sounds to detect them early.
  • A single shot from any firearm kills a headcrab.

Variants

In later Half-Life games, additional headcrab variants appear (fast headcrabs, poison headcrabs). Only the standard headcrab appears in Half-Life 1.

Quick Facts

  • First enemy encountered in the game.

  • Cannot attach to Gordon (HEV suit protection).
