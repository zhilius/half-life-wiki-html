Overview

Assassins are female covert operatives deployed by the Black Ops division after the HECU marines. Dressed in black jumpsuits, they are extremely agile, can perform acrobatic flips, and are deadly at close range with their knives. They represent a significant escalation in the military response.

Behavior

Assassins are extremely fast and agile. They perform backflips and wall jumps to evade fire and close distance. They attack with powerful knife strikes that deal heavy damage. Their speed makes them difficult targets to hit. Assassins work in pairs or small groups, coordinating their attacks.

Combat Strategies

  • The shotgun is effective when they close range.
  • Keep distance — their knives are deadly up close.
  • Use automatic weapons to track their movement.
  • Listen for their distinctive acoustic signature.
