Overview

The Bullsquid is a large, aggressive alien predator found in the later sections of Black Mesa. It has a tentacle-covered face and attacks by spitting corrosive acid and biting with its sharp teeth. Bullsquids are highly territorial and will aggressively pursue intruders.

Behavior

Bullsquids patrol areas aggressively. They spit green acid that deals damage over time and can hit from a surprising distance. At close range, they use a powerful biting attack. They have a distinctive roar and their footsteps are heavy and audible. They often roam in specific areas and will pursue the player across rooms.

Combat Strategies

  • Keep moving to avoid acid spit.
  • The shotgun is very effective at close range.
  • Use doorways as chokepoints to limit their mobility.
  • The acid deals damage over time — avoid standing in it.
