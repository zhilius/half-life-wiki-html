Overview

The Gonarch is a massive, headcrab-like creature encountered in Xen. It is a boss-level enemy that the player cannot avoid. The Gonarch spawns smaller headcrabs from its hanging abdominal sack and attacks with melee swipes. It is believed to be the source of many of the headcrabs that invaded Earth.

Behavior

The Gonarch chases the player through a series of caverns in Xen. It periodically stops to spawn headcrabs from its sack. The battle takes place across multiple arenas as the player lures the Gonarch through tunnels. It is vulnerable to all weapons but has enormous health.

Combat Strategies

  • Focus fire on the Gonarch's head or sack.
  • Kill spawned headcrabs quickly to avoid being overwhelmed.
  • Use the Tau Cannon (charged shots) for high damage.
  • Keep moving — the Gonarch is fast for its size.
