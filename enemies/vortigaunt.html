Overview

The Vortigaunt is a sentient alien species from Xen, enslaved by the Nihilanth to serve as soldiers in the invasion of Earth. They are intelligent beings capable of speech and tool use. In combat, they fire crackling green energy bolts from their single arm. Later in the series (Half-Life 2), some Vortigaunts become allies to humanity.

Behavior

Vortigaunts fire slow-moving energy bolts at the player. They take cover when possible and are dangerous in groups. Their energy bolts can be dodged due to their slow speed. In melee range, they will use a claw attack. They make distinctive charging sounds before firing.

Combat Strategies

  • Dodge their slow energy bolts while closing distance.
  • The shotgun is highly effective at close range.
  • Use cover to avoid multiple energy bolts from groups.
  • Listen for their charge-up sound to time your dodges.
