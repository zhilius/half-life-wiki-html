Overview

The Hazardous Environment Combat Unit (HECU) are elite U.S. Marines deployed to Black Mesa after the resonance cascade. Their official mission is to contain the alien threat, but their actual orders are to silence all witnesses — including Black Mesa personnel. They are intelligent, well-armed, and use military tactics.

Behavior

Marines use realistic combat tactics: they take cover, flank the player, throw grenades, and call out targets. They will retreat to heal and coordinate attacks. Squad leaders are identifiable by their radio packs. Some marines wield RPGs or shotguns. They are the most dangerous human enemy in the game.

Combat Strategies

  • Use the .357 Magnum to penetrate their body armor.
  • Flank them — they expect frontal assaults.
  • Catch grenades thrown at you and throw them back.
  • Use the crossbow for silent elimination of sentries.
