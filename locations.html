Location Overview

Half-Life takes place primarily within the Black Mesa Research Facility, a massive underground complex in the New Mexico desert. The game also features the alien dimension Xen in its final chapters. Each location has a distinct atmosphere and set of challenges.

Black Mesa Research Facility

Sector D — Anomalous Materials Lab

The starting area where the resonance cascade occurs. Contains the test chamber, H.E.V. suit room, and surrounding laboratories. Connected to the broader facility via tram system and service corridors.

Office Complex

Executive and administrative offices, cubicles, conference rooms, and the cafeteria. The alien invasion caused chaos here first. Bullsquids and headcrabs roam these areas.

Blast Pit — Rocket Test Facility

An underground rocket engine test chamber. Features a massive rocket on a gantry system. The Tentacle creature is encountered here. The rocket engine is used as a weapon against the creature.

Lambda Complex

The most advanced research section of Black Mesa. Home to the Lambda Team, who were studying teleportation technology. Contains the prototype teleporter used to reach Xen. Protected by laser grids and heavy security.

Surface Level

The outer areas of Black Mesa exposed to the surface. Includes a dam, helipad, storage yards, and supply roads. HECU forces have established forward operating bases here. The Apache helicopter patrols these areas.

Underground Transit / Rail System

A network of railway tunnels and maintenance areas connecting Black Mesa's various sectors. Used for cargo transport. Patrolled by both aliens and military forces.

Xen

Borderworld Overview

Xen is an alien dimension that exists between worlds. It features floating islands, bizarre biology, and a purple-green sky. The environment is low-gravity and hazardous. The Nihilanth rules Xen from its central stronghold.

Gonarch's Lair

A series of interconnected caverns where the Gonarch dwells. The walls are organic and pulsing. Headcrabs spawn here continuously.

Interloper — Vortigaunt Factory

A massive organic structure where Vortigaunts are forced to work. Contains conveyor belts, grinding machinery, and alien industrial equipment. The Nihilanth's slave labor camp.

The Borderworld Core

The Nihilanth's throne room. A vast organic chamber at the heart of Xen. This is where the final battle takes place. After the Nihilanth's defeat, the chamber begins to collapse.

Location Facts

  • Black Mesa is based on real facilities like Los Alamos and Area 51.
  • The facility spans hundreds of acres underground.
  • Xen's low gravity affects gameplay significantly.
  • The Lambda Complex contains the only working teleporter.
