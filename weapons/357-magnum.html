Overview

The .357 Magnum is a high-powered revolver found in the Office Complex. It fires large-caliber rounds that deal massive damage per shot at the cost of a slow rate of fire and scarce ammunition. One of the best weapons for taking down armored HECU marines.

Usage

The .357 excels at taking down priority targets with well-placed shots. Its high damage per round makes it efficient against HECU marines, as it penetrates their body armor. However, ammunition is limited throughout the game, so each shot should count.

Combat Tips

  • Use against HECU marines — it penetrates their armor effectively.
  • Conserve ammunition for tough enemies only.
  • Headshots are devastating with this weapon.
  • Slow fire rate makes it unsuitable for crowds.

Quick Facts

  • Holds 6 rounds in the cylinder.
  • Highest damage-per-shot of any firearm.
  • Ammunition is rare throughout the game.
  • Effective against armored enemies.
