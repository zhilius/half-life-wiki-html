Overview

The Satchel Charge is a remote-detonated explosive device. Players can place satchels on walls, floors, or ceilings and detonate them remotely using the secondary fire button. Multiple satchels can be placed and detonated simultaneously.

Weapon Specifications

Satchel Charge Stats
CategoryExplosive (Remote)
Damage120 per satchel

RangeUnlimited (detonation)
First FoundChapter 6: Blast Pit

Usage

Place satchels strategically in enemy patrol routes or near objectives. The ability to detonate multiple satchels at once makes them excellent for creating large traps or destroying tough enemies. Use primary fire to place a satchel, secondary fire to detonate all placed satchels.

Combat Tips

  • Place multiple satchels on a single target for massive damage.
  • Use as a trap by placing satchels around corners.
  • Detonate from behind cover to avoid splash damage.
  • Satchels can be shot to detonate prematurely.

Quick Facts

  • Can place up to 5 satchels simultaneously.
  • All satchels detonate at once on secondary fire.
  • Useful for ambushes and traps.
  • Can destroy breakable walls.
