Overview

The Crossbow is a high-precision sniper weapon found in the second half of Half-Life. It fires metal bolts over long distances and features a laser sight for pinpoint accuracy. Bolts can pin enemies to walls, a satisfying and macabre detail.

Usage

The crossbow fires a single bolt per shot with a manual reload. The laser sight projects a red dot showing exactly where the bolt will hit, making it extremely accurate at long range. Bolts travel in a slight arc with gravity affecting their trajectory over very long distances.

Combat Tips

  • Perfect for eliminating distant HECU marines before they detect you.
  • The laser sight can give away your position — use carefully.
  • Bolts are retrievable from surfaces (but not from bodies).
  • Headshots with the crossbow are instantly fatal against most enemies.

Quick Facts

  • One of only two weapons with a scope/laser sight.
  • Bolts can pin enemies to walls.
  • Requires a manual reload after each shot.
  • Deals 100 damage per hit.
