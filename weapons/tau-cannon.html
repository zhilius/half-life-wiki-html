Overview

The Tau Cannon (also known as the Gauss Gun) is an advanced energy weapon developed by Black Mesa's Lambda Team. It fires a high-energy Tau particle beam that can be charged for increased damage by holding the fire button. Found in the Lambda Complex, it is one of the most powerful weapons in the game.

Usage

The Tau Cannon has two firing modes. The primary fire fires a quick, low-damage bolt. The secondary fire allows the player to charge the weapon — holding the button builds up energy, releasing for a powerful blast. A fully charged shot can kill most enemies in one hit. The weapon also allows for a technique called "rail jumping" where players use the recoil to launch themselves across gaps.

Combat Tips

  • Charge shots for maximum damage against tough enemies.
  • Uncharged shots are useful for conserving energy against weak foes.
  • The recoil can be used for advanced movement (rail jumping).
  • Energy cells are rare in Xen — conserve when possible.

Quick Facts
