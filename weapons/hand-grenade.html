Overview

The Hand Grenade is a standard M67 fragmentation grenade used throughout Half-Life. Players can throw grenades by pressing the fire button. Holding the fire button "cooks" the grenade — it detonates shortly after release, allowing for timed throws.

Usage

Grenades are effective against groups and enemies behind cover. The "cooking" mechanic allows the player to hold the grenade, and the fuse timer continues to tick. Release at the right moment for airburst detonations or to prevent enemies from escaping. HECU marines will sometimes throw grenades back if they see them.

Combat Tips

  • Cook grenades to prevent enemies from running away.
  • Use against HECU marines in fortified positions.
  • Grenades bounce off surfaces — bank them around corners.
  • Watch out for enemies throwing grenades back!
