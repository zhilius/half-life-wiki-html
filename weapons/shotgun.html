Overview

The Shotgun is a lever-action 12-gauge shotgun found early in Half-Life. It is one of the most versatile and effective weapons in the game, devastating at close range and capable of killing most enemies in one or two shots.

Usage

The shotgun excels at close-quarters combat. Its wide pellet spread makes it forgiving but ineffective at range. The secondary fire fires both barrels simultaneously for double damage, at the cost of two shells. This is devastating against tough enemies like Vortigaunts and Zombies.

Combat Tips

  • Use the double-barrel blast (secondary fire) for tough enemies.
  • Aim for center mass — the spread will do the rest.
  • Reload after every encounter to keep the magazine full.
  • Switch to the Glock or MP5 for ranged combat.

Quick Facts

  • Holds 6 shells in the tube magazine.
  • Secondary fire fires both barrels.
  • Effective against all enemy types at close range.
  • Shells are plentiful throughout the game.
