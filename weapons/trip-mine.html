Overview

The Trip Mine is a laser-triggered explosive device. When placed, it projects a thin red laser beam. Any entity crossing the beam triggers a powerful explosion. The mine can be picked up and reused if disarmed carefully.

Usage

Place trip mines in doorways, corridors, or anywhere enemies are likely to pass. The laser beam is visible, so observant enemies may avoid it. Approach a placed mine and press use to pick it up safely. The explosion is powerful enough to kill most enemies instantly.

Combat Tips

  • Place at ankle height where enemies are less likely to see the laser.
  • Create cross-fire traps with overlapping lasers.
  • Can be picked up and repositioned.
  • Explosions can trigger other explosives chain reaction.

Quick Facts

  • Laser is visible — use placement creatively.
  • Can be picked up safely with the use key.
  • Also damages the player if caught.
  • Effective against HECU patrols.
