Overview

The Snark is a living alien organism used as a biological weapon. When deployed, the snark skitters around and attacks any living creature — friend or foe. They are found in the later chapters of Half-Life and are retrieved from Xen specimens.

Usage

Deploy snarks by throwing them like grenades. They will immediately seek out the nearest living target and attack. After a period of time, they explode. Snarks do not distinguish between enemies and the player — they will attack Gordon if no other targets are available.

Combat Tips

  • Throw multiple snarks for maximum chaos.
  • Use as a distraction while you flank enemies.
  • Back away after throwing to avoid friendly fire.
  • Particularly effective against Vortigaunts and HECU marines.
