Overview

The RPG (Rocket-Propelled Grenade Launcher) is a heavy weapon found in the middle sections of Half-Life. It fires self-guided rockets that track the player's aim point, making it effective against distant and moving targets. Essential for taking down the Apache helicopter and the Gargantua.

Usage

The RPG fires a rocket that follows the player's crosshair. To guide the rocket, the player must keep the target within their view. The rocket travels relatively slowly, allowing for course correction during flight. The splash damage is significant and can damage the player at close range.

Combat Tips

  • Essential for the Apache helicopter encounter in Chapter 9.
  • Use against the Gargantua to stun and defeat it.
  • Maintain distance — the backblast and splash damage are lethal.
  • Rockets are rare — save them for mandatory targets.

Quick Facts

  • Rockets are laser-guided by the player's aim.
  • Only 5 rockets can be carried at once.
  • One of the few weapons effective against the Apache.
  • Splash damage can hurt the player.
