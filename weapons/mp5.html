Overview

The MP5 Submachine Gun is the primary automatic weapon in Half-Life. Chambered in 9mm, it offers a high rate of fire and shares ammunition with the Glock 17. A distinctive feature is its under-barrel M203 grenade launcher, capable of firing high-explosive grenades in an arc.

Usage

The MP5 is the workhorse weapon of Half-Life. Its high magazine capacity and fully automatic fire make it effective against groups of enemies. The secondary fire mode fires a grenade from the under-barrel launcher, useful against clusters of enemies or around corners.

Combat Tips

  • Use controlled bursts at medium range for accuracy.
  • The grenade launcher is invaluable against HECU marines in cover.
  • Save grenades for armored enemies or groups.
  • Watch your ammunition — 9mm rounds are shared with the Glock 17.

Quick Facts

  • The MP5 holds 50 rounds per magazine.
  • It features an integrated M203 grenade launcher.
  • Grenades can bounce off walls (cook by holding fire).
  • It shares ammunition with the Glock 17.
