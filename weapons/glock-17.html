Overview

The Glock 17 is the first firearm acquired in Half-Life. Found on a security guard shortly after the resonance cascade, this 9mm semi-automatic pistol serves as a reliable backup weapon throughout the game. It is manufactured by the Glock company of Austria.

Usage

The Glock 17 is accurate and reliable. It shares ammunition with the MP5, making it a practical sidearm. Its semi-automatic fire allows for precise shot placement, and headshots are effective against most human enemies. The weapon is best used at medium range against isolated targets.

Combat Tips

  • Aim for headshots against human enemies for maximum efficiency.
  • Use against Headcrabs to conserve MP5 ammunition.
  • Switch to the Glock for long-range precision shots.
  • Reload frequently — 17 rounds deplete quickly in combat.

Quick Facts

  • The Glock 17 uses a 17-round magazine.
  • It shares ammo with the MP5.
  • Headshots deal double damage.
  • It is the second weapon found in the game.
