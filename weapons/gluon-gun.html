Overview

The Gluon Gun is the single most powerful weapon in Half-Life. It projects a continuous beam of glowing energy that disintegrates anything it touches. Found in the Xen chapters, it consumes energy cells at an alarming rate but deals devastating damage to everything in its path.

Usage

The Gluon Gun fires a continuous beam as long as the fire button is held. It drains energy cells rapidly — a full charge of 100 energy lasts only a few seconds. The beam can sweep across multiple enemies, and its high damage output makes short work of even the toughest foes including Alien Controllers and the Nihilanth.

Combat Tips

  • Use in short bursts to conserve energy cells.
  • Sweep the beam across groups of enemies for maximum efficiency.
  • Ideal for the Nihilanth boss fight.
  • Shares ammunition with the Tau Cannon — manage your energy carefully.
