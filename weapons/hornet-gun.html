Overview

The Hornet Gun is a biological alien weapon obtained from a defeated Vortigaunt in the Xen chapters. It fires living hornets that track enemies with homing capabilities. Its unique feature is that ammunition regenerates over time, making it an infinite-use weapon.

Usage

The hornet gun has two firing modes. Primary fire launches hornets directly. Secondary fire launches hornets that home in on targets. The self-regenerating ammunition makes this weapon invaluable in Xen where supplies are scarce.

Combat Tips

  • Use homing fire against fast-moving enemies.
  • An excellent backup weapon — it never truly runs out.
  • Effective against Xen native creatures.
  • Regeneration is slow — pace your shots.

Quick Facts

  • Ammunition regenerates over time.
  • Has a homing secondary fire mode.
  • Dropped by Vortigaunts.
  • Only available in the final chapters.
