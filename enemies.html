Enemy Overview

Half-Life features a diverse array of hostile creatures and forces. The enemies can be broadly categorized into Xen aliens (creatures from the borderworld), HECU military forces, and Xen bosses.

Enemy Notes

  • Headcrabs are the most common enemy encountered.
  • HECU marines use similar weapons to the player.
  • The Nihilanth is the only required boss fight.
  • Barnacles can be used as improvised elevators.
